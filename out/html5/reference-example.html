Features

This reference page details some of the additional exciting features of Desert Bus for Android.

Horn

You may beep the horn of the bus at any time during your drive by tapping the center of the steering wheel.

Rearview Mirror

To view what is going on in the back of the bus, see the rearview mirror in the top right of your screen. Note: nothing is ever going on in the back of the bus.

Clock

To view the time of day, see the real time clock in the bottom right corner of your screen.
